Catering

If you are interested in something that’s not listed on this website, having an upcoming party catered or would simply like to place an order, please contact me. I am able to accommodate most requests.

Please provide at least 5 days notice for catering.

I can offer suggestions as to the types of dishes, and there is usually a minimum charge of €10.00 per person (this can vary depending on what’s been ordered).

In addition, I can also provide waiter service for an hourly fee of €20.00.

Workshops

Workshops are also available for a minimum of 6 persons. Please provide at least 2 weeks notice.

Workshops are approx. 4 – 5 hours and include dinner and drinks (including Lebanese wine).

Please email me for more information.
